Jose G. Generoso

Senator Jose G. Generoso was a professor of law before he entered politics. He was Representative from the District of Manila to the Fourth Philippine Legislature (1916-1919). In 1928, he was elected Senator from the fourth Senatorial District comprising Bataan, Rizal, Manila and Laguna and served up to 1934.

After the Senate, he was appointed Technical Assistant and Special Counsel, Office of the President of the Philippines (1935-1938). He was also Judge of the Court of Industrial Relations.
