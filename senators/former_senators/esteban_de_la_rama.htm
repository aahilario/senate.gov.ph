Esteban de la Rama

Senator Esteban de la Rama was educated in the Philippines and abroad.

He was general in the Philippine Revolutionary army in 1898. He was one of the most outstanding businessman in the Visayas, being manager of the De la Rama Central and owner of M/S Don Esteban.

He was second Vice President of the Asociacion de Los Veteramos de la Revolucion and commanding officer of the 6th Division of the National Volunteers of the Philippine Islands with the rank of Major General.

He served as Senator for two terms : 1941-1945 and 1946-1947.
