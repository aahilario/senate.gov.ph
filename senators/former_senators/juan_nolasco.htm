Juan G. Nolasco

Senator Nolasco was born in Tondo, Manila on March 8, 1885 to Ceferino Nolasco and Severa Gomez.

He acquired his Bachelor of Arts degree from the Ateneo de Manila, and his Licentiate in Medicine and Surgery from the University of Santo Tomas.

He was a consulting physician of the Mary Johnston Hospital for many years and elected municipal councilor of Manila from 1916 to 1919. Senator Nolasco was elected Representative from the district of Manila from 1919 to 1922 and became a Senator from the fourth senatorial district from 1931 to 1935.
